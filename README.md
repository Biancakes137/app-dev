# Stranger Things

## 📺 Overview
**Stranger Things** is a science fiction horror drama series set in the 1980s. It follows a group of kids in Hawkins, Indiana, as they uncover supernatural mysteries, government conspiracies, and a dimension known as the *Upside Down*.

- **Genre**: Sci-Fi, Horror, Drama  
- **Created by**: The Duffer Brothers  
- **First Aired**: July 15, 2016  
- **Streaming on**: Netflix  

## 🌟 Main Characters
- **Eleven (Millie Bobby Brown)** – A girl with psychokinetic powers.
- **Mike Wheeler** – Loyal and intelligent friend of Eleven.
- **Jim Hopper** – Chief of police, protector of Eleven.
- **Will Byers** – First boy taken by the Upside Down.
- **Dustin & Lucas** – Funny and smart, part of the core friend group.

## 🎯 Why I Love It
- Blends 80s nostalgia with thrilling storytelling  
- Strong emotional depth and character development  
- Mind-bending supernatural mysteries  

## 🔍 Favorite Episode
**Season 3, Episode 8 – “The Battle of Starcourt”**  
This finale is packed with action, emotion, and surprises. It wraps up key arcs and sets the stage for the next season.
